​The Myth of the Ship of Memory Concept

​ In our memory, we bring the past to life. Based on it, we construct images of the future. We forget, deconstruct, or supplement and reconstruct our memories. We wander through our memories in search of answers to the challenges of the present or future prospects. All these possibilities of memory testify to its extraordinary plasticity and, at the same time, its significant role in shaping human essence. This means that it is possible to study memory at the level of such plastic human qualities as sensations and intuition, i.e., at the level of art. 

The goal of the art project «The Myth of the Ship of Memory» is to find a reference point for infinity, which is located in our present and opens up paths to the past and future. 

By paying attention to how the focus changes in the process of remembering, long-forgotten images emerge. Over time, the focus of our attention changes under the influence of external or personal circumstances, and new horizons of the future are outlined. By exploring memory as a plastic material, the narrative of which changes throughout the existence of its carrier, a person or society, we see how narrative memory is, how it unfolds in time not only in the past but also captures the future. We see how the spatial-temporal orientation of memory can change vectors and, accordingly, transport the carrier into a different, new reality. 

In developing the concept of an artistic and imaginative study of memory, we turn to the experience of creating a myth that exists in a specific space, has its origins, driving forces, and finale, as well as a central figure who occupies a special place. The model of the Ship of Memory, which travels in the ocean of time between the «Morning Star» and the «Evening Star», is built on the principle of myth. These two figures symbolize the beginning of the day and the beginning of the night — basic elements that simultaneously reflect the repetitiveness and diversity of the cyclical nature of the space-time in which memory exists. 

Attempting to conquer and direct the course of the Ship, the figure of the «Helmsman» stands at the stern. Just as consciousness can evoke images of the past or distance itself from them, so He steers the ship, which sometimes picks up speed and sometimes slows down, passing along the paths of the past or opening up new horizons of the future. At the same time, the driving force of the ship is its rowers — images of emotional states that stimulate the process of remembrance. These states are two opposites: calm and peace, and tension and longing. They evoke our thoughts about the past and the future, or immerse us in these dimensions, even against our will. The rowers of the Ship of Memory are four figures of thinkers: «Under the Sun», «By the River», «Midnight Reflections», and «In Prison». One only has to succumb to their influence, and then the rowers accelerate the Ship's movement through the expanses of time that open up in our memory. 

However, no matter what paths the Ship takes, it always only approaches the future. The figurehead of the ship, «Trumpeting Angel», symbolizes the inevitability of the future. He is always ahead and indicates that every path taken has its own result and logical conclusion. A special place in the compositional ensemble of figures is at the intersection between them in the center. The viewer is invited to take this place and become the central figure of the project, filling the myth with their own images and guiding the Ship of Memory along their paths. 

Andriy Nikiforov
